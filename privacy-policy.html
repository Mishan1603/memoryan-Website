Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our commitment. We will never compromise your trust.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Memoryan. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Your privacy is our priority, and we believe in transparency about our data practices.

2. Data Storage Philosophy

Memoryan is designed with privacy in mind. By default, all your content (chambers, blocks, notes, and media) is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or have access to your personal content unless you explicitly enable synchronization features.

3. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Email address and authentication information when you register.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for compatibility.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage patterns to improve app functionality.
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous error reports to fix bugs and improve stability.
  • Notification Preferences: Your notification settings and scheduling preferences for deadline reminders.

4. Synchronization & Cloud Storage

When you enable synchronization:

  • Your content is encrypted and stored securely in our cloud servers.
  • Data is transmitted using industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL).
  • You can disable sync at any time, which will delete all cloud data.
  • Sync is entirely optional and disabled by default.

Without sync enabled, your data never leaves your device.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Authenticate your account securely.
  • Sync your data across devices (only if enabled).
  • Send deadline reminders and review notifications (only with your permission).
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance functionality.
  • Communicate important updates or security notices.
  • Provide customer support when requested.

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Period.

Limited third-party services we use:

  • Supabase: For secure authentication and optional cloud storage.
  • Crash reporting services: For anonymous error tracking.
  • Analytics services: For anonymous usage statistics.

These services are bound by strict privacy agreements and cannot access your content.

7. Data Security

We implement robust security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission.
  • Secure local storage using platform-specific encryption.
  • Regular security audits and updates.
  • No plain-text storage of sensitive information.
  • Secure authentication with industry standards.

8. Notifications & Permissions

Memoryan uses notifications to help you stay organized:

  • Deadline Reminders: Notifications about important deadlines you set for your blocks.
  • Review Reminders: Optional notifications to help you review content at optimal times.
  • Learning Schedule: Reminders for your study and learning schedule.

We will only send notifications after you grant permission. You can:

  • Enable or disable notifications at any time in device settings.
  • Customize notification frequency and timing in the app.
  • Control which types of reminders you receive.
  • Completely turn off all notifications without affecting other app features.

9. Your Rights & Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all data we have about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct your information.
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data.
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format.
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics and crash reporting.
  • Sync Control: Enable or disable cloud synchronization.
  • Notification Control: Manage all notification preferences and permissions.

10. Data Retention

Local data: Stored on your device until you delete it.

Cloud data: Stored only while sync is enabled.

Account data: Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.

Notification data: Settings and preferences deleted when you disable notifications.

Analytics: Anonymous data retained for up to 2 years.

You can request immediate deletion of all data at any time.

11. Children's Privacy

Memoryan is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

12. International Users

Memoryan is available globally. If you use our app outside your country of residence, your data may be transferred to and processed in other countries. We ensure the same level of protection regardless of location.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: info@mymemoryan.com

Through the app: Use the feedback feature in Settings.

We are committed to addressing your privacy concerns promptly and transparently.

Your privacy is our commitment. We will never compromise your trust.
